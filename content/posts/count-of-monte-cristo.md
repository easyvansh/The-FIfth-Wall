Modern self-help suggests that changing your life is a pleasant optimization problem. Wake up at five, journal, optimize your nutrients. But Alexandre Dumas understood that true transformation requires a death. Edmond Dantes must disappear before the Count can emerge.

> Only a man who has felt ultimate despair is capable of feeling ultimate bliss.
